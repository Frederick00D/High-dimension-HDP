These notebooks reproduce the numerical experiments in the paper for private Gaussian mean release under the power-divergence family. 
simulation_avgnorm.ipynb studies normalized MSE, while simulation_supnorm.ipynb studies sup-norm exceedance probabilities, both under AR(1) and heterogeneous covariance designs. 
In both experiments, the Hellinger choice λ=−1/2 performs best, supporting the paper’s conclusion that it is the least restrictive Gaussian calibration in high dimension.
